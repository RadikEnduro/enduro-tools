Ray Perrault
Fieldhouse.

A civic fieldhouse that took a public park from "okay" to "destination" over 16 weeks.

Chapter 01

The brief.

The City wanted a fieldhouse that would last 30 years and read modern. Public-facing, durable finishes, washrooms and changerooms, a covered gathering space. Budget tight, schedule tighter.

Chapter 02

The build.

16 weeks. We pre-fabricated wall assemblies off-site to compress the on-park schedule, then phased the install around the soccer league. The park stayed open the whole time.

Chapter 03

The opening.

Opened on the booked date. The park hosted its first community event a week later — a soccer tournament, with families clustered under the covered gathering space. The fieldhouse did its job.
