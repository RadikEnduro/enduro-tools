Chapter 01 · Brief

A storefront with brand on its mind.

Norgate came to us with a strong brand and a tight schedule. The build had to read like the brand, open on the booked date, and pass health-authority inspection without a return visit. The previous tenant left a complicated demo problem behind the drywall.

Chapter 02 · Approach

Permit-aware, brand-loyal.

We coordinated permitting in parallel with demo, brought the architect's millwork specs in-house, and held a Wednesday standup with the brand team for 10 weeks straight. Every finish sample was approved twice — once on paper, once in the room.

Chapter 03 · Build

Quiet work in a noisy neighbourhood.

The store sits on a busy retail strip. We ran the heavy work between 9pm and 4am for the first three weeks, then transitioned to standard hours once the loud trades were done. The neighbours never complained.

Chapter 04 · Handover

Open on time. Inspection clean.

One inspection. One deficiency walk before the invoice. The store opened on the day they posted the launch on social, with a punch list closed to zero.
